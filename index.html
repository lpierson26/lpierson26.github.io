About The Shop

Locker Room Legends has been the heart of the Cedar Rapids and Iowa City sports community for over 30 years. Owned and operated by Dan & Tanya Miller, we specialize in high-end collectibles across the MLB, NBA, NCAA, NFL & NHL.

Whether you're looking for vintage wax packs, autographed memorabilia, or fan cave decor, we bring authentic sports history right to the Lindale Mall.
