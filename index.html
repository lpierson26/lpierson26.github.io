About The Legends

Locker Room Legends has proudly served the Cedar Rapids and Iowa City area for over 30 years. Owned and operated by Dan & Tanya Miller, our retail store is located in the lower level of Lindale Mall in Cedar Rapids, Iowa — right behind Chick-fil-A.

Everything we sell is related to MLB, NBA, NCAA, NFL & NHL. We offer sports cards, wax packs, autographed memorabilia, vintage collectibles, artwork, fan cave decor, apparel, kitchen items, tailgating gear, and much more.
